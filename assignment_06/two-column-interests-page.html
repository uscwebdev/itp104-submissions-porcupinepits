Beautiful scenery.

Hiking up the mountains is rewarding in itself, for once you reach your destination, you're bound to have an amazing view.

Leave in time to make it to the sunset or sunrise, you won't regret it!

Hiking is a great way to make friends!

There are many hiking groups out there! Reach out and find people to hike with, who knows? Maybe you'll make lifelong friends. It's a common hobby amongst many groups of people!

Or, you can even take your own friends and family hiking. You can never get tired of hiking because there's just too much to explore! :]

The real reward is in the memories you'll make along the way.

Don't forget to take pictures, or have something to remember each moment. One day you'll look back, and you will appreciate all of the memories that you made during these hikes.

Moments live temporarily, but the memories will always last. Keep each memory in a photograph.
